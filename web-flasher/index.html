Orion Dial.

Independent, community-built project. Not affiliated with, endorsed by, or supported by Orion Sleep or Waveshare.

A bedside dial for your Orion Sleep dual-zone topper — turn a knob to change the temperature of your side of the bed. Flash it straight from this page; no software to install.

Your browser can't flash over USB. Open this page in Chrome or Edge on a desktop computer — Safari and Firefox don't support the Web Serial API this needs. Web Serial is blocked on this page. Try again in a normal browser tab (not embedded in another app).

Works in Chrome or Edge. Not Safari or Firefox.

Before you click

  • Use a USB-C data cable (not a charge-only cable), plugged straight into your computer — not through a hub.
  • When the picker asks you to choose a port, pick it, then hit Connect.
  • If this is a brand-new dial, choose the option to erase the device before installing, for a clean slate.
No port shows up? Flip the plug.

This board's single USB-C port wires its two data-pin pairs to two different chips. One plug orientation reaches the main ESP32-S3; flipped 180°, it reaches a companion chip instead. If no device appears in the picker — or the wrong one does — unplug the cable, flip it over, and plug it back in. You want the port that shows up as an ESP32-S3 or "USB JTAG/serial debug unit".

After flashing

Everything else happens on the dial's own screen: Wi-Fi setup, pairing with your Orion account, choosing which side of the bed it controls. Future firmware updates arrive over the air, straight from this project's GitHub Releases — you won't need this page again unless you get a new board.
