## `orion_root_ca.pem`

- **What:** A concatenated bundle of public CA root certificates, embedded
  for on-device TLS verification (see
  `firmware/dial-idf/components/dial_oauth/orion_root_ca.pem`).
- **Note:** These are public root certificates published by their issuing
  certificate authorities for exactly this purpose (verifying TLS chains).
  Redistributing them for that purpose raises no license concern.
